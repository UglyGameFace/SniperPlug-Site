About SniperPlug

Deal alerts with proof, not noise.

SniperPlug is a retail deal-discovery platform built to help shoppers find verified markdowns, promotions, price drops, and product availability from trusted retailers.

What we do

We organize deal alerts into store pages, category pages, and deal detail pages that link shoppers back to official retailer product pages.

What we avoid

No checkout automation, no account automation, no retailer impersonation, and no resale of retailer data. SniperPlug is built as a publisher and deal-discovery experience.
